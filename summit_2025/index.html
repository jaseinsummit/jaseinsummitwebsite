The 2025 summit

The second edition of JASEIN (Junior Academics in Strategy, Entrepreneurship, and Innovation in the Netherlands) will be held on Januari 23rd & 24th, 2025. It will be held at Hotel de Leijhof in Oisterwijk, and is sponsored by the University of Tilburg. On the first evening, we will have dinner and drinks at the hotel's excellent restaurant The George, to be followed with a day of interesting speaker, panels, and workshop sessions. We look forward welcoming the JASEIN community to Noord-Brabant!
