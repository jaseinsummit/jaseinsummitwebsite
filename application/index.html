Application information

You can apply using this Google Form.

The deadline for applying is October 20th, 2023. We aim to inform accepted applicants by mid-November 2023. For questions, please send an email to jasein.summit@gmail.com.

Costs

Thanks to the generous sponsoring of the Amsterdam Business School, participants need only to pay for transportation and accommodation. In case you want to join us for a stay at this beautiful castle between the 25th and 26th of January, you will have to pay a (reduced) fee for the stay. The rate is 148.45 euros (including breakfast and city tax).
