Application information

Applications are open! You can apply using this Google Form.

The deadline for applying is midnight October 31st, 2025. To allow for interactive discussions in a friendly environment, there will be a limited number of spots available. There are no specific criteria for selection, but we look for synergy in research interests and a fair balance across affiliated universities. All participants are expected to be Assistant Professors based in the Netherlands. For questions, please send an email to jasein.summit@gmail.com.

Costs

Thanks to the generous sponsoring of the departments of Organization studies and Strategy and Entrepreneurship of Tilburg University, participants need only to pay for transportation and accommodation. The JASEIN organization committee will inform attendees regarding accommodation options on-site or within walking distance of the venue.
