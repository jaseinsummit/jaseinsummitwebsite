Hotel de Leijhof

Hotel de Leijhof in Oisterwijk will ensure an exciting JASEIN summit where we establish new connections, engage each other meaningfully, and generate useful insights. The town of Oisterwijk is known for its quaint center full of bars and restaurants, and its regenerated industrial area, the KVL Fabriek. This complex is home to the bakery of Robèrt van Beckhoven, highly aclaimed master baker and pastry chef, and part of the presenter team of 'Heel Holland Bakt' (the Dutch version of the 'Great Britisch Bake Off'). Make sure to visit the bakery and try some his bread and pastries, they are a true delight!

Directions

Oisterwijk has its own train station, which has a direct train connection with Tilburg and Eindhoven. Alternatively, there is a direct bus connection from train stations Tilburg and Den Bosch. As Oisterwijk is near Tilburg, renting an OV-bicycle at Tilburg is a feasible option (8.6 km). From the Oisterwijk train station, it is a 800m walk to our venue.
