Introducing JASEIN

JASEIN (Junior Academics in Strategy Entrepreneurship and Innovation in the Netherlands) is a network for Assistant Professors based in the Netherlands working in the field of strategy, entrepreneurship, and innovation. Our primary goal is to cultivate a vibrant academic network and foster valuable connections among early-career researchers. Beyond the exchange of ideas, the network also embraces a social dimension, providing opportunities for informal interactions and shared experiences that enrich both our academic pursuits and personal sense of belonging in Dutch academia.

JASEIN 2024

The first edition of the JASEIN summit will be held at the beautiful castle Kerckebosch. We look forward to welcoming you on January 25th at 16:00 for drinks and a dinner reception, followed by a full day of social and professional development on January 26th (9:00-18:00). Event expenses are paid by this year's host, University of Amsterdam Business School, so participants need only to pay for transportation and accommodation. We have rooms reserved at a reduced price at Kasteel Kerckebosch; please indicate in your application if you'd like us to reserve a room.
