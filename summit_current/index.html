The 2026 summit

The third edition of JASEIN (Junior Academics in Strategy, Entrepreneurship, and Innovation in the Netherlands) will again be held on February 12th & 13th, 2026. It will be held at Kasteel Kerckebosch in Zeist, and is sponsored by the Rotterdam School of Management. On the first evening, we will have dinner and drinks at the hotel's excellent restaurant, to be followed with a day of interesting speakers, panels, and workshop sessions. We look forward to reuniting the JASEIN community again!
