The concept

   Why

Academia can seem like an intransparent system, especially Dutch academia, and especially for the growing group of early career researchers who just came to the Netherlands. The solution to that is to band together and meet the faces behind the system. Our primary goal is to cultivate a vibrant academic network and foster valuable connections among early-career researchers as well as inviting key players in the Dutch academic system. Beyond the exchange of ideas, the network also embraces a social dimension, providing opportunities for informal interactions and shared experiences that enrich both our academic pursuits and personal sense of belonging in Dutch academia.

   For whom

A powerful network requires a sufficient amount of shared interest. Therefore, JASEIN focuses on Assistant rofessors because their juniority in, and commitment to, academia makes them interested in a deeper understanding of the system they will be working in. JASEIN focuses on researchers in business strategy, entrepreneurship, and innovation in recognition of a degree of discipline specificity to the challenges in academia. Even more specifically, JASEIN recognizes the unique institutional environments in which academia can be embedded, and therefore focuses on academics employed at a Dutch university.

   Activities

The main activity organized by JASEIN is the summit. The summit consists of activities that are informative and interactive, professional and social.

Organizers

JASEIN is organized by representatives from the University of Amsterdam, Tilburg University, Vrije Universiteit of Amsterdam, and Erasmus University.
