17:00 - 18:00 Check-in & Cocktail Reception
18:00 - 00:00 Dinner and evening of socializing

January 24th

9:00 - 9:15 Welcoming remarks by JASEIN Organizing Committee
9:15 - 10:30 Working with Dutch Data
An interactive panel discussion with scholars from the Dutch SEI community about the use and opportunities of employing 'Dutch' data.
10:30 - 11:00 Coffee Break
11:00 - 12:30 Rapid Connections: Avenues for Future Collaborations
Networking activity to meet fellow 'JASEIN' scholars to learn about them and their work, as to foster sustainable connections.
12:30 - 13:30 Lunch
13:30 - 15:00 Making the Most Out of a Bad Situation: Responses to Changes in Dutch Academia
A panel discussion with Pedro de Faria (Groningen), Joris Knoben (Tilburg), Vareska van de Vrande (Rotterdam), and Tine Buyl (Tilburg).
15:00 - 15:30 Coffee Break
15:30 - 16:30 Life as a Dutch Academic: Roundtables and Pantomime
Roundtable discussions on different themes of being an academic in the Netherlands followed by a group exercise.
16:30 - 17:00 Closing remarks by JASEIN Organizing Committee
