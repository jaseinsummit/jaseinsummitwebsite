The concept

   Why

Academia is rife with uncertainty, particularly for early career researchers. Our primary goal is to cultivate a vibrant academic network to foster information exchange, stimulate research collaboration, and offer social support to Assistant Professors working in the Netherlands.

   For whom

A powerful network requires shared interests. With this objective in mind, JASEIN focuses broadly on researchers working in the fields of strategy, entrepreneurship, and innovation. JASEIN also recognizes the unique institutional environments in which academia can be embedded and thus, focuses on junior academics employed at a Dutch university.

   Activities

The main activity organized by JASEIN is the annual summit to gather Assistant Professors in an informal environment. The summit consists of activities that are informative and interactive, professional and social.

Organizers

JASEIN is currently organized by representatives from the University of Amsterdam, Tilburg University, Vrije Universiteit of Amsterdam, Erasmus University Rotterdam, and the University of Groningen. To ensure having a diverse reflection from the Dutch academic JASEIN community, we try to involve representatives from different Dutch universities over time.

Founders

JASEIN was founded by representatives from the University of Amsterdam, Tilburg University, Vrije Universiteit of Amsterdam, and Erasmus University Rotterdam.
